Because we don't all have to take the picture.

partyShots is a photo sharing platform that allows you to upload and share photos with friends and family.

It's easy to use and free to sign up.

How it Works

  • 1. Create an account

    It's simple and free.

  • 2. Create an album

    They just need a name

  • 3. Upload photos to the album

    Just enter a description and select the files

  • 4. Share the album code with friends and family

    hint: Albums can also be found by usernames

  • 5. Other people can add photos to the album

    Anyone who is signed in can upload to any album

    You can remove photos from any album you created

  • 6. View and download photos from the album

Important Information

All images are openly public to anyone with the album code, the URL of the album, or a username.

There is no assumption of privacy.
